Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

ICF Insider ("we," "us," or "our") operates icfinsider.com. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and who we share it with. We keep this simple and honest — no legal boilerplate designed to confuse.

1. What We Collect

When you submit a project request through our "Get Connected" form, we collect:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number
  • Your project type and a description of your project
  • Your state or location
  • Any project files you choose to upload (PDF, DWG, images, ZIP — up to 100MB)
  • Any file links you provide (Google Drive, Dropbox, WeTransfer, etc.)

We also collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) for security and analytics purposes. We do not use tracking cookies or sell advertising.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit to:

  • Connect you with qualified ICF contractors in your area
  • Follow up with you about your project request
  • Improve how the site works

We do not use your information to send unsolicited marketing emails or sell you anything directly.

3. Who We Share Your Information With

When you submit a project request, your contact details and project description are shared with ICF contractors or distributors relevant to your location and project type. This is the core service — connecting you with people who can actually build your project.

We do not sell your information to data brokers, advertisers, or unrelated third parties.

We use the following infrastructure to store and process your data:

  • Cloudflare (D1 database and R2 file storage) — your submitted data and uploaded files are stored on Cloudflare's infrastructure

4. How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain submitted project requests and any uploaded files until they are no longer needed to facilitate contractor connections, or until you ask us to delete them. We do not have a fixed automatic deletion schedule at this time.

5. Your Rights

You can contact us at any time to:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Ask us to delete your data

To make a request, email us at privacy@icfinsider.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

ICF Insider is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the site grows. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top. Material changes will be noted clearly.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at privacy@icfinsider.com.
