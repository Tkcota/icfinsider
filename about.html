About ICF Insider

Built by Someone Who Sees the Problem Every Day

ICF Insider exists because the industry has a gap — and homeowners are paying for it. We built this to fix that.

Who We Are

ICF Insider was founded by Tyler Klein, an ICF industry professional who has been working in the insulated concrete form space since 2022. Over the past several years, Tyler has worked across multiple sides of the industry — from web development for ICF businesses to supporting ICF training groups and hands-on involvement in building ICF homes.

That breadth of experience across distribution, education, and construction gave Tyler a clear view of both what ICF can do and where the industry consistently falls short.

Why We Built This

The problem is simple: ICF is one of the most compelling building methods available to homeowners today — more durable, more energy efficient, and more disaster-resistant than traditional wood frame construction. And yet, homeowners who want to build with ICF run into the same wall over and over again.

They can't find a qualified builder in their area.

Nearly every homeowner Tyler speaks with — and it's a daily conversation — asks the same three questions: How much does it cost? What are the pros and cons? And where do I find someone who actually knows how to build with it? That last question is the one that stops most projects before they start. Without a local builder, a homeowner who was genuinely interested in ICF ends up choosing a different building method by default — not because ICF wasn't right for them, but because the support wasn't there.

ICF Insider was built to close that gap. A single place where homeowners can get real cost data, compare the major brands independently, and most importantly — connect with qualified ICF professionals in their area. And a place where ICF contractors, builders, and distributors can actually be found.

Our Commitment

ICF Insider is fully independent. No manufacturer funds this site, no brand pays to be ranked higher, and no listing is promoted because of who makes the blocks. The cost data, brand comparisons, and contractor directory exist to serve homeowners and the professionals who build for them — not to push a particular product.

The directory is built to connect serious homeowners with qualified ICF professionals across North America. Our goal is to build the most complete, most trusted ICF resource on the internet — and that starts with making sure every qualified builder, distributor, and designer has a place to be found.
