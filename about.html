About ICF Insider

Built by Someone Who Sees the Problem Every Day

ICF Insider exists because the industry has a gap, and homeowners are paying for it. We built this to fix that.

Who We Are

ICF Insider was founded by Tyler Klein, who has spent several years working inside the ICF distribution industry — helping contractors grow their businesses and educating homeowners on the benefits of ICF construction. That experience on both sides of the transaction, the contractor trying to grow and the homeowner trying to build, is what led directly to this site.

Working inside ICF distribution means daily conversations with homeowners who are serious about building but stuck. The same questions come up every time. The same roadblocks stop the same projects. After years of watching that pattern repeat, the gap became impossible to ignore.

Why We Built This

The problem is straightforward: ICF is one of the most compelling building methods available to homeowners today — more durable, more energy efficient, and more disaster-resistant than traditional wood frame construction. And yet homeowners who want to build with it run into the same wall over and over again.

They can't find a qualified builder in their area.

The questions are always the same. How much does it cost? What are the real pros and cons? And where do I find someone who actually knows how to build with it? That last question is the one that kills most projects before they start. Without a local builder to call, a homeowner who was genuinely interested in ICF ends up choosing a different building method by default — not because ICF wasn't right for them, but because the support simply wasn't there.

ICF Insider was built to close that gap. A single independent resource where homeowners can get real cost data, compare the major brands without manufacturer bias, and connect with qualified ICF professionals in their area. And a place where ICF contractors, builders, and distributors can actually be found by the people looking for them.

Our Commitment

ICF Insider is fully independent. No manufacturer funds this site, no brand pays to be ranked higher, and no listing is promoted because of who makes the blocks. The cost data, brand comparisons, and contractor directory exist to serve homeowners and the professionals who build for them, not to push a particular product.

The directory is built to connect serious homeowners with qualified ICF professionals across North America. Our goal is to build the most complete, most trusted ICF resource on the internet, and that starts with making sure every qualified builder, distributor, and designer has a place to be found.
