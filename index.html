The Basics

What Is ICF Construction?

Insulated Concrete Forms (ICF) are hollow foam blocks that stack like Legos, then get filled with reinforced concrete. The result is a structure that's stronger, quieter, and far more energy-efficient than conventional wood framing.

ICF homes routinely achieve 50–70% lower heating and cooling costs. They're rated to withstand hurricane-force winds, wildfire, and flooding. They've been built across North America for 30+ years — the technology is proven, the contractors are here, and the economics are better than ever.

  • Disaster Resistant Rated for 200+ mph winds. Fire, flood, and earthquake resistant.
  • Long-Term Savings 50–70% lower energy bills. Pays for itself in years, not decades.
  • Quiet & Comfortable Exceptional sound dampening. Consistent indoor temperature year-round.
  • Proven Technology 30+ years of builds across North America. Thousands of contractors trained.
